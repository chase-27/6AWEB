About

We believe in transparency, cooperation globally, and the free exchange of talent and ideas. Our forums provide opportunities for meetings, conferences and other events. We are committed to creating a secure, constructive and supportive atmosphere for all participants.

Conferences are part of the legacy of this organization and continue today to be a catalyst for creativity. We are committed to developing strategies that support and expand conference activities throughout the world.
